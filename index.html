  • Walkaton

    I participate to Walkaton which is a fundraising event where participants walk a predetermined distance, often raising money for a charitable cause through sponsorships and donations.

  • The Hopeful Disease

    Won-jun has a sister who is living with type 1 diabetes. He supports his sister as she deals with daily struggles with diabetes. He hopes that other your children who have siblings with type 1 diabetes acquire better understanding of type 1 diabetes by reading this book.

  • Autism Race, Autism Speaks Korea

    The Autism Racing campaign is designed to support people with autism through racing. Specifically, Autism Racing donates a certain amount of money calculated from the total time and distance I have participated in running. This running campaign acknowledges the challenges and sufferings faced by autism patients and the obstacles they have to overcome. By voluntarily participating in this campaign, I was able to donate funds equivalent to 10,000 walks to support autistic children.

    The main objective of the 'Project Eco8' was to deploy an application that promotes eco-friendly practices and enhances the relationship between the residents of Jeju Island and the island’s beautiful natural environment. By leveraging technology and innovation, we aim to provide a user-centric and intuitive application experience that fosters sustainable behaviors.
