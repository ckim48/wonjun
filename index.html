Gallery

Photography is more than just a hobby for me; it's a way of living. Through the lens of my camera, I capture the world's moments, emotions, and stories, turning ordinary scenes into extraordinary narratives frozen in time.

Pond and Temple

The stone temple in the middle of the pond caught my attention, mainly because the pond’s surroundings are mostly empty, with no other background landmarks present.

Building Forest

The buildings are orderly placed with a structured pattern and design. Most of them feature teal glass facades, which caught my attention as it reflects the modern trend in architectural design.













Slums become an artwork

The buildings in the photo are weathered structures, construced over 50 years ago, now serving as residential dwellings for numerous city residents.




Oak Forest

This photo captures an expanse of trees surrounding the forest, adorned with lush leaves. It serves as a powerful inspiration for me to actively participate in environmentally friendly projects dedicated to conserving and preserving the Earth.





Korean Traditional Architecture

This image is significant for inspecting my architecture because it showcases the detailed shape aumentation inside the rooftop and the ceilings, arranged in a sequential order of linear shapes. The linear shape fascinates me as I ponder how those lines are formed perfectly within the roof.

The interior design incorporates a blend of traditional and modern architectural elements. Among the traditional features are the dynasty colored pillar and jade colored ceiling, which represent the beautry of East Asian architecture.

Temples have been located throughout Korean mountains since before the Middle Ages. This architecture showcases the advancement of Korean traditional architecture, which holds spiritual value by following Buddhist principles.

Seoul’s Sky

The visual appeal of Seoul’s sky is most notable during sunny days when the hills and buildings are visible. However, this aesthetic quality is significantly compromised by the city’s high levels of air pollution.









Shapes

This photo emphasizes the linear edges of the white hotel building and the rectangular shape within the pool. The straight lines create a picturesque scene resmebling a beach view from the swimming pool.
